Company Origins

Founded and incorporated on 12th November 1962 as F. Fisher Investments Limited, we established our core values from day one; to continually strive to be the best and to exceed our clients expectations by providing an honest, flexible and innovative approach in project delivery, as well as treating people with trust and respect. With over 60 years’ experience of delivering high quality building projects, construction and real estate, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional people spaces. London & District Investments Limited delivers a 360 perspective in this fast-moving environment and we always challenge convention, drive innovation, and surpass expectations. Our dedicated team ensures each project, from concept to completion, is provided with an individual and flexible service, ensuring that we are adding value and efficiencies without compromising on service or quality. We act as the catalyst in bringing our client’s vision to life.

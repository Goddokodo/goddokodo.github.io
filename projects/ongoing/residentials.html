Residential Apartments, Bugoolobi


A London&District flagship project: This stunning residential project involves the establishment of multiple apartments at the center of Bugoloobi in Kampala, Uganda fully powered with solar energy and HEP.

Detailed works to both the interior and exterior of these buildings will give the place a new lease of life. Our comprehensive package of works includes the creation of six 30 bedroom apartments. All apartments will be fitted with high specification finishes throughout.
